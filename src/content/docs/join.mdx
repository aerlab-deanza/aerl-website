## Prospective members

If you want to join the lab, be ready to say:

- what discipline or skills you bring
- which committee interests you most
- whether you want hardware, firmware, controls, simulation, or documentation work
- how much time you can commit each week

## Sponsors

If you want to support the lab, useful sponsorship areas include:

- flight hardware and spare parts
- sensors, batteries, and power electronics
- fabrication materials
- software, analysis, or testing tools

## Collaborators

We welcome collaborators who can help with:

- control systems and estimation
- embedded software and validation
- simulation workflows
- technical review
- publications and open-source release quality
