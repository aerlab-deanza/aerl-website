## Recruitment

The lab is building across four committee areas:

- Controls and Dynamics
- Hardware and Fabrication
- Electronics and Power
- Simulation and Software

Members who want hands-on experience in embedded systems, control theory, firmware, fabrication, or data analysis should use the [Join and Contact page](/join/).

## Sponsors

Potential sponsors can help with:

- hardware procurement
- test equipment
- fabrication support
- software and analysis tooling
- travel, showcase, or competition costs if applicable

## Collaborators

We are interested in collaborators who can contribute:

- domain expertise
- mentorship
- research guidance
- validation support
- publication and open-source review
