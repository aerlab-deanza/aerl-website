## Mission

The Applied Engineering Research Lab exists to:

- Execute ambitious engineering projects
- Develop engineering talent
- Foster interdisciplinary collaboration
- Contribute to open knowledge through publications and open-source work
- Bridge academia and industry

## Vision

We envision a lab where theory from class is applied directly to ambitious projects, failures become structured learning, and students leave with stronger technical judgment than they arrived with.

AERL is where you build full-stack engineering systems. We derive equations, simulate systems, and build real prototypes.

## Values

- Rigor in testing, validation, and documentation
- Collaboration across disciplines and committees
- Ambition in project scope and technical depth
- Openness through publications, repositories, and public writeups
